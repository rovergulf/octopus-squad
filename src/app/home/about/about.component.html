About us

Octopus Squad is a good deeds NFT collection of 10k octopuses generated programmatically using 150 hand-drawn elements.

We wish to create a community that is not indifferent to the fate of our planet, nature and its inhabitants, as well as to unite people interested in NFT.

Part of the funds will go to charitable foundations for cleaning water bodies and helping animals, supporting and developing the community, its members and future projects.
